Shared coffee

What is shared coffee ?

Shared coffee is a campaign aimed to help homeless people by sharing with them coffee and giving them a warm place

Who are we

We are 3 students who decided to create this campaign .

This campaign began in Lyon, but it will extend and expand to help more people .

How to participate ?

if you are interested you can contact one of the coffee shops in the map page and pay a coffee
